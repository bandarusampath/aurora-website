Our Facilities

Institution is equipped with various facilities like Canteen, Library, Laboratories, Transportation, Yoga And Meditation, Auditorium, Playground, Parking Yard, WiFi connectivity

The library has 34,200 volumes of books pertaining to various subjects, 5,350 titles, a collection of CDs of SONET, EMPTL, and CDs of textbooks, e-journals. It also receives 2,000 above on-line journals through by DELNET

The Institution is equipped with a large ground for Outdoor sports, parking, transportation of vehicles and trees.

Tasty And Healthy Food

Our institution has spacious canteen facility in the college premises. A variety of south indian food and snack items are provided to the students. Adequate seating facilities are provided to accomodate students and staff even during peak time. The Canteen has a big kitchen with steam cooking facility and kitchen staff take extra care to maintain hygiene. Food items are prepared with RO treated water.
